# Using AS5600 Magnetic Rotary Position Sensor

## AS5600 Board

AS5600 I2C Address: 0x36 0b0110110

Vcc - Red  
Out - Orange  
Gnd - Black  

Dir - Yellow  
SCL - Green  
SDA - Blue  
GPO - WHite

## R-Pi Board

40 - VBUS - Red  
38 - GND - Black  

1 - GP0 - SDA - Green  
2 - GP! - SCL - Blue  
3 - GND - GND

8 - GND  
9 - GP6 - I2C1 SDA -  
10 - GP7 - I2C1 SCL -
